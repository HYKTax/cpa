Pricing FAQ

Questions about how we bill.

Are fees really flat?
Yes. Once we scope your engagement, you receive a written proposal with a fixed fee. If the scope changes materially during the engagement, we'll flag it and discuss before continuing — no billing surprises.
What's included in the monthly bookkeeping fee?
Everything in the tier's feature list — bank/credit reconciliation, transaction categorization, financial statements, and your chosen level of support. Year-end tax return is separate (priced flat by return type).
Do I have to sign an annual contract?
Monthly engagements are month-to-month with 30-day notice. Annual plans save you 15% but lock in the rate for 12 months. One-time engagements (returns, formations) are pay-on-completion.
Are discounts available?
Annual pre-payment saves 15% on recurring services. Bundled engagements (e.g., monthly bookkeeping + annual return + quarterly advisory) receive a 10% package discount. Non-profits receive 15% off standard pricing.
What payment methods do you accept?
ACH (preferred, no fee), credit card (3% processing fee), and wire transfer for engagements over $5,000. Annual plans are paid in full upfront; monthly plans auto-draft on the 1st.
What if my return is more complex than the starting price?
The "From" price reflects a straightforward return for that type. Complexity factors (rental properties, K-1s, multi-state, foreign accounts, crypto activity) add clearly-disclosed fees. You'll see the full quote before we begin.
