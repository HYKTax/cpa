Security First

Built with the rigor your data actually requires.

Your tax data is among the most sensitive information about your life and business. We treat it accordingly — with controls audited annually by independent third parties.

AES-256 Encryption

Bank-grade encryption protects every document in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Your CPA can read it. Nobody else can.

SOC 2 Type II

Our portal infrastructure is independently audited annually for security, availability, processing integrity, and confidentiality.

Two-Factor Auth

Required for every account. Use SMS, an authenticator app, or a security key. Your password alone is never enough.

IRS Pub. 4557

Full compliance with the IRS data security framework for tax professionals — including incident response and breach reporting.
