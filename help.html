Frequently Asked
How long does it take to get a response from my CPA?
Within your service plan: same business day on Growth, 4 hours on Enterprise, and 48 hours on Essentials. For urgent matters, call our office directly.
Where can I find my prior-year returns?
Go to Returns → Filed. Every return we've ever prepared for you is there with PDF download.
How do I update my billing or payment method?
Go to Settings → Billing to update your saved payment methods, billing address, or invoice delivery preferences.
How do I add a family member or business partner to my account?
Spouse and shared business partner access can be granted from Settings → Access. Each person gets their own login with appropriate permissions.
