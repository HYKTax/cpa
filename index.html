Frequently Asked

Questions we hear all the time.

Didn't see your question? Send us a note — we usually reply within a few hours.

Are you a licensed U.S. CPA firm?
Yes. HYK Tax is led by a Florida-licensed CPA registered with the Florida Board of Accountancy. All federal returns are signed by a credentialed preparer (CPA or IRS Enrolled Agent) with a valid PTIN.
How does the U.S./India model actually work?
You work directly with your U.S.-based CPA for all planning, review, and strategic decisions. Our offshore team in Surat handles data entry, reconciliation, and return preparation overnight — which is why our turnaround times are faster than most domestic-only firms. Every deliverable is reviewed and signed off by the U.S. CPA before it reaches you.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use encrypted client portals (TaxDome, Box), SOC-2 compliant software, and strict access controls. We never share or store documents outside of approved systems, and all team members sign confidentiality agreements. We comply with IRS Publication 4557 data security requirements.
What does engagement cost?
We use transparent flat fees based on scope, not hourly billing. Individual returns start at $295, monthly bookkeeping at $395/month, and quarterly advisory at $1,250/quarter. After your discovery call, you'll get a written proposal — no surprises.
Do you handle multi-state or multi-entity situations?
Yes, routinely. We file multi-state returns, manage sales tax nexus across all 50 states, and handle complex structures including holding companies, consolidated returns, and cross-border K-1s. Tell us the scope on the discovery call and we'll confirm it's a fit.
Can you work with my existing QuickBooks / software stack?
Yes. We work natively in QuickBooks Online & Desktop, Xero, Gusto, ADP, Stripe, Shopify, Amazon Seller Central, and most major platforms. Our tax prep uses CCH Axcess, GoSystem Tax RS, UltraTax CS, ProConnect, and Drake — whichever fits the engagement best.
What if I get audited?
We stand behind every return we sign. Our Enrolled Agents are federally authorized to represent you before the IRS for audits, appeals, and collections. Representation in routine notice matters is included; complex audits are scoped separately as a flat-fee engagement.
